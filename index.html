Erin designs delightful user experiences
that inspire collaboration.

Currently working at a government incubator, Erin leverages over 15 years of experience in national security to accelerate the adoption of industry-leading capabilities to advance mission imperatives. Her passion for user-driven design is breaking cultural barriers to innovation, one prototype at a time.

“Through design thinking, we remember the value of play as a gateway to possibility.”

I’m a multi-disciplinary designer based in Northern Virginia and have spent my career connecting the dots between mission, intelligence, and technology to accelerate government innovation. The convergence of disruptive technologies offers endless opportunity to create and learn from others, and I am passionate about empowering you to do the same through design.

Previous lives in visual art, language, and research have all shaped my interest in user-centered design, and while I look to past experience for inspiration, I draw on human interactions and empathy to create meaningful experiences and solve problems.
